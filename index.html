Crystal Hwang

Crystal Hwang is currently a student at Brigham Young University, studying Information Systems. She is from the suburbs of Chicago and enjoys traveling the world. Read below to learn more about her education, interests, hobbies, and achievements!

Education

Crystal attended high school at Oswego East in Illinois and graduated in 2021. She was a member of the National Honor Society, Rho Kappa, Mu Alpha Theta, the National Technical Honor Society, and various other clubs. She was captain of and a 4-year varsity player on both the tennis and badminton teams. She graduated with a 4.7 GPA and was accepted into Brigham Young University, where she is currently a student.

Crystal will be graduating this April with a bachelor's in Information Systems and a double minor is Computer Science and Cybersecurity. She was a member of the BYU Racquetball Team in 2022, while working with the BYU Football recruiting department as an intern. In 2023, she completed an internship with the Detroit Pistons and the Utah Jazz, working in their IT and Marketing departments, respectively. She is currently a second-year intern for BYU Athletics, working in the Digital department, while also a technology intern for Lane VC.

Interests and Hobbies

Crystal has many interests and hobbies, including cooking and baking whenever possible, especially for her friends and family. She enjoys traveling the world and experiencing new cultures, foods, and sights. She enjoys weighlifting and working out, as well as doing diamond painting while watching a fun show. She prioritizes spending time with her family and friends, and loves to do fun activities with them whenever possible.

Achievements

As mentioned before, Crystal graduated from high school with over a 4.7 GPA and in the top 10% of her class. She received a one-year scholarship award from Brigham Young University because of her academic exellence from her freshman year of college. She has completed various internships and was a member of the BYU Racquetball Team. Her greatest achievement is being an aunt to five beautiful nieces and nephews.
